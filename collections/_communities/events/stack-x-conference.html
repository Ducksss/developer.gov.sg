STACK-X Conference

STACK-X Conference on CI/CD (SHIP-HATS).

Join us on a 2-day hybrid conference to learn about Singapore Government's vision, approach and programs to help agencies to accelerate their modernisation and digitalisation plans with CI/CD. The conference spotlights on SHIP-HATS - the CI/CD component in Singapore Government Tech Stack.

Who should attend?

We have tailored content for

  • Industry practitioners especially those working on public sector projects

  • Agency Product Managers, Business Analysts, Developers, Solution Architects

  • Agency CIOs and IT Directors

Why attend?

Modernise, securely

Learn how CI/CD with SHIP-HATS built in security, while supporting modern development practices.

Hear from Agencies and Industries

Tune in to get trends and practical lessons from agencies like ICA and PA and industry players like ThoughtWorks and NCS including live Q&A.

Learn from planning to onboarding

Wide range of sessions to help agencies and vendor community understand SHIP-HATS and navigate processes - such as planning, budgeting, tendering - to adopt the product.

Day 3: May 7th

SHIP-HATS Developer Workshop

 

This free hands-on workshop is a in-person session at Suntec is for developers or practitioners especially those working on public sector projects. In this session, you will learn how to build and release a container application using CI/CD pipeline with dependency management and vulnerability assessment to Government Commercial Cloud (GCC).

 

The session covers:

  • Continuous Integration with dependency management using Nexus solution and automated container scanning using Plasma Cloud. [1.5 hours]

  • Release Management to promote release artifacts to production with secure deployment to GCC compartments. [1 hour]

 

This session is open to agencies, industry and developer communities. Limited seats!

We have lined up a series of sessions both in-person and online for CIOs to learn how they can build in security, while adopting modern development practices.  

 

Due to safe distancing policies, we can only invite 30 participants join us in person at Suntec. You can register and tune in right from your desk to listen to the Keynotes, Tracks: Get to Know SHIP-HATS and Steps to Adopt SHIP-HATS.
