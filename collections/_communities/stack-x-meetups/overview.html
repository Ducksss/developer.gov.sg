Overview

The STACK-X Meetup community is a platform for everyone who is passionate about using technology for public good. It is hosted on Meetup and provides an avenue for everyone to share their views and feedback, learn from experts and fellow techies and join fruitful discussions on the use of technology for public good. Past webinars have covered topics such as Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), Open Policy Agent (OPA) and can be accessed through this link.

You can also join our STACK-X Meetup community through this link. to receive notifications about meetups.
